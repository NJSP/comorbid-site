What is Comorbid Interactive?

    Comorbid Interactive is an independent game studio formed in 2024 by N.J. Petrone.
    I like to focus on creating games with an emotional and cerebral impact that stick 
    with players long after they step away from the keyboard.

Who is N.J. Petrone?

    I'm an aspiring software engineer and indie game developer.
    I started my journey in game dev in March of 2024 when I decided I 
    needed a break from studying for the Comptia Security+ exam. In 
    retrospect I probably took years off my life by trying to learn the 
    fundamentals of cybersecurity while also learning how to navigate 
    Unreal Engine and program in Blueprints, but I guess I just like
    to push myself.
